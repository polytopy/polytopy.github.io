Our Solutions

We specialise in developing robust computer vision and SLAM technologies that enable machines to perceive, understand, and navigate the world with precision.

Computer Vision

Real-time simultaneous localisation and mapping using advanced visual odometry algorithms. Our SLAM solutions provide accurate pose estimation and dense 3D reconstruction for autonomous navigation.

Robotics

End-to-end robotic solutions from perception to motion planning. We develop robust control systems and navigation stacks that enable autonomous operation in dynamic environments, combining our expertise in SLAM, path planning, and real-time systems.

AI / ML

State-of-the-art object detection, tracking, and scene understanding. We develop custom vision algorithms optimised for robotics, automotive, and industrial applications.

Sensor Integration

Multi-sensor fusion and intelligent perception pipelines that combine camera, LiDAR, Radar, IMU, and GNSS data for robust environmental understanding in challenging conditions.
